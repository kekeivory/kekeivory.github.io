KeShawn Ivory, MS


Hello! My name is KeShawn Ivory and I'm an astrophysics PhD candidate at Vanderbilt University. I currently work on the relationship between galaxy dynamics and supermassive black hole merger timescales. The most important aspects of astronomy to me, though, are any and all measures to ensure that anyone who wants to join this field can do so, and that those who decide not to can still access all the discoveries we're making.
